## Mechanism / UM
- mechanisms referenced: Carta 1300 / Mobius flexible screen; 300 PPI; AI text conversion (as-you-write / selected / full-page modes); Float PICKING button for cross-app annotation; 960Hz stylus report rate, 0.05mm displacement precision; 7 file-transfer methods; calendar sync (Google/Outlook); built-in 4G (Reader); Kaleido 3 color (Reader C).
- UM type played: primarily Feature UM — upgrades the recognized e-ink-tablet category on specific features (thinnest 4.5mm, +15% clarity / +20% refresh, 960Hz stylus, AI-on-page). Secondary Product UM on the Reader line ("distraction-free E-Ink screen that's easy on your eyes" — e-ink as the mechanism vs backlit screens). No Problem UM (does not argue a novel cause of the problem).

- market sophistication stage this brand plays at: Stage 4. The e-ink-tablet category and the Product UM ("e-ink = no blue light, no glare, no eye strain") are already established by Boox, reMarkable, Kindle Scribe. Viwoods competes on feature-level superiority — thinnest (4.5mm), +15% clarity, +20% refresh, 960Hz stylus, AI conversion modes, built-in 4G — plus heavy deal/value framing and trust stacking. Quantified comparative claims and price-anchored discounts are classic Stage 4 behavior.
