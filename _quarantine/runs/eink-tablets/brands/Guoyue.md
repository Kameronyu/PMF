## Product
- Smartbook T1 — 7" mono E Ink, 1680×1624 / 300 PPI, flush glass bezel, 29-LED
  white+amber frontlight, quad-core 1.8 GHz, 2 GB RAM / 32 GB, 2300 mAh, BT 5.1,
  USB-C, Android 8.1 with sideloading. Bundled stylus (4096 pressure levels,
  USB-chargeable, eraser/nav buttons). Price: ~$298–339 on AliExpress; $399 all-in
  via authorized resellers (Good e-Reader Store, case + stylus).
- Smartbook V5 / V5 Color — 7.8" E Ink Kaleido Plus color, 1872×1404 / 300 PPI mono
  layer + 624×468 / 100 PPI color layer, 4096+ colors, 36-LED frontlight, quad-core
  1.82 GHz, 2 GB / 32 GB, 2800 mAh, Wacom-style stylus (4096 pressure, 8 colors),
  palm rejection, Android 8.1 (Onyx Boox-style OS skin). Price: ~$375–530 across
  listings. White-labeled equivalent of the Bigme S3 ($899).
- Other variants seen in listings: K3 Color 10.3" (JDRead-branded), "READ" e-reader.
- Format: standalone Android e-note / e-reader hardware. No app, no subscription.

## Transformation(s)
- No marketed transformation in the DR sense — listings are spec sheets, not promises.
  Implicit outcomes inferred from feature copy and reviewer framing: paperless
  note-taking, distraction-free reading, eye-comfort vs. backlit screens.
- Reviewer-attributed value frame (Good e-Reader): "a ton of value" — a color e-note
  at roughly half the price of the Bigme S3. The de facto transformation is
  "get a color e-note / e-reader cheaply," i.e. price arbitrage, not outcome change.
