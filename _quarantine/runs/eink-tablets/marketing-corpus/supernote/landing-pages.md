### FAQ (verbatim)
- Q: Can I use it to watch movies and play video games? — A: "No, Supernote is not an 'All-in-one' tablet computer and isn't intended for everyone. Its design focuses on writing and reading to stimulate creative thinking, and nothing more. We suggest considering the iPad as an alternative."
- Q: Why is it more expensive than others? — A: "Because Supernote's long-term value is superior to others."
- Q: Why no front/backlight? — A: "We completely understand why a frontlight/backlight is a valued feature in other e-readers, especially for low-light reading, and how convenient it can be. At Supernote, our core philosophy is centered around providing the absolute best handwriting experience possible – we truly believe in 'for those who create (write & sketch).' To achieve this, a deliberate design choice was made to forgo a frontlight/backlight..."
- Q: Why no color? — A: "This involves trade-offs against the optimal handwriting experience. Sadly, we are not taking the plunge into color ink technology until it is further developed since the present color ink screen has low reflectivity which causes a dim display, poor color differentiation, and the need for frontlight, which also has an effect on the handwriting experience. For the optimum handwriting experience, we must make a compromise."
- Q: What formats does Supernote support? — A: "Supernote Manta and Nomad currently support Note (.note), PDF, EPUB, Word, Text (.txt), PNG, JPG, WebP, CBZ, FB2 and XPS, and integrate with Kindle app which supports MOBI and other formats."
