## Discount triggers (verbatim)

### Student Discount Program — `/pages/student-discount-program`
On-page WebFetch returned navigation only; details below sourced from support article `https://support.supernote.com/en_US/my-order/student-discount`:
- Discount: "University/college students can save 20 USD on Supernote devices"
- Eligibility: "The discount program allows students from more than 6,000 schools in lots of countries to use their valid university email addresses"
- Verification: "We partner with a third party to verify your eligibility" (vendor name not specified on the page; for Hero program GovX is named — likely same stack)
- Flow: "Just click 'Get Discount' and follow the prompts to verify your status to receive a one-time discount code that can be used at checkout"
- Edge cases: "Email not recognised?" link; spam-folder/university-server troubleshooting; allow up to 12 hours; contact service@supernote.com if delayed.
- Recovery: If unshipped, request refund with verification email/code screenshot or cancel to reorder. If shipped, code usable on future purchase.
