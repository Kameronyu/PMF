### PR-1: Launch announcement (verbatim — full release)

URL: https://www.prnewswire.com/news-releases/mudita-introduces-the-kompakt-an-e-ink-minimalist-phone-designed-for-digital-well-being-302429925.html
Dateline: WARSAW, Poland, April 16, 2025 /PRNewswire/
Headline: "Mudita Introduces the Kompakt: An E Ink, Minimalist Phone
Designed for Digital Well-Being"

Body (verbatim):

"Mudita, a leader in mindful technology, announces the launch of the
Mudita Kompakt, a minimalist mobile phone designed to help users reclaim
their focus and well-being. As digital distractions continue to grow, the
Mudita Kompakt offers a streamlined, clutter-free experience that
supports a more balanced lifestyle.

Tech That Supports Your Well-Being

The Mudita Kompakt is built for those who want to minimize screen time
and develop a more thoughtful approach to their tech habits. With a
distraction-free interface, E Ink screen, and custom-built operating
system, it provides essential phone functions without unnecessary digital
noise, making it easier to stay focused and engaged in daily life away
from their devices. The Mudita Kompakt's features are thoughtfully
designed to reduce stress and promote personal growth by encouraging more
conscious technology use.

Reimagining Productivity and Balance

Mudita understands that staying connected shouldn't come at the cost of
focus and efficiency. By reducing digital overwhelm, the Mudita Kompakt
helps users achieve better work-life integration, enabling them to be
more productive in their professional and personal lives without
distractions such as constant notifications pulling them away.

[CEO QUOTE]
Mudita's CEO Michal Stasiuk states: 'Technology should enhance our lives,
not take over them. With the Mudita Kompakt, we're helping people break
free from digital distractions so they can focus on what truly matters to
them; whether that's work, relationships, or personal growth.'

Expanding Mudita's Portfolio of Wellness-Focused Technology

The Mudita Kompakt joins the brand's growing portfolio of wellness-driven
products, including the Mudita Harmony 2 and Mudita Bell 2 alarm clocks,
as well as the Mudita Moment and Mudita Element minimalist watches. Each
product is crafted with a deep understanding of digital wellness,
empowering users to set their own boundaries between tech and real-world
experiences.

Availability

The Mudita Kompakt is available for pre-order now at Mudita.com at a MSRP
of $439 (manufacturer's discounts apply up to 20% on all pre-orders).
North American shipments will begin in May. Currently, Mudita is
launching the Global version for regions outside of North America. For
more information, visit Mudita.com and follow Mudita on Instagram at
@wearemudita."

### PR-2: NA shipping & pre-order close (verbatim — full release)

URL: https://www.prnewswire.com/news-releases/mudita-kompakt-north-america-optimized-to-begin-shipping-next-week-final-days-for-special-pre-order-pricing-302457291.html
Dateline: WARSAW, Poland, May 16, 2025 /PRNewswire/
Headline: "Mudita Kompakt North America Optimized to Begin Shipping Next
Week; Final Days for Special Pre-Order Pricing"

Body (verbatim):

"Mudita, a mindful technology company from Poland, is excited to announce
that its Mudita Kompakt (North America Optimized), a minimalist phone
crafted to simplify digital life and enhance mindful living, is scheduled
to begin shipping next week to crowdfunding backers in the United States
and Canada. This marks a final opportunity for customers in these regions
to secure the device at the special pre-order price of $369 before
regular pricing takes effect once shipping commences.

The Mudita Kompakt (North America Optimized) begins shipping next week to
early adopters in the U.S. and Canada; offering a distraction-free phone
experience designed for intentional living.

Winner of the iF Design Award and Red Dot Award 2025, the Mudita Kompakt
features a glare-free E Ink screen and minimalist design tailored to
support digital wellbeing.

This launch builds upon the significant community support demonstrated
during its successful crowdfunding campaigns on platforms like
Kickstarter and Indiegogo (over $500k pledged).

Mudita Kompakt - Designed for Intentional Living

Mudita Kompakt is specifically engineered to help users disconnect from
the constant digital distractions that define modern life. This award-
winning device, recognized with accolades such as the iF Design Award
2025 and the Red Dot Award: Product Design 2025, is built around
essential features that foster mindfulness and intentional technology
use. With a distraction-free interface, glare-free E Ink screen, custom-
built operating system, and long battery life, the Mudita Kompakt has
been met with positive recognition from reviewers and users, who praise
its unique approach to technology and minimalist design.

North American Pre-Order Information and Upcoming Shipping

For customers in the United States and Canada, the North America
Optimized version of Mudita Kompakt is available at the special pre-order
price of $369 until shipping begins next week. The North America
Optimized version is specifically tailored to US and Canadian cellular
network bands. This version is strongly recommended for the most reliable
connectivity and performance in these regions.

14-Day Satisfaction Guarantee

Once received, customers will have 14 days to try out their Mudita
Kompakt. If it's not the right fit, it can be returned in undamaged
condition for a full refund for the device and any returned add-ons.

Global Optimized Version Already Available

For customers outside of North America, or those who prefer the global
band configuration, the Mudita Kompakt (Global Optimized) has already
completed its pre-order phase and is currently available worldwide at its
regular price of €439 / $439.

About Mudita

Mudita, headquartered in Warsaw, Poland, is committed to creating
technology that promotes simplicity, mindfulness, and digital wellbeing.
Mudita's award-winning products, including the Mudita Kompakt, and Mudita
Harmony 2 alarm clock, have gained international recognition for their
innovative approach to intentional technology usage. Mudita continues to
lead in developing solutions designed to foster meaningful connections
and a balanced lifestyle."
