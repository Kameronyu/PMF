## Product

- Device price: **$399** one-time (+ optional $9.99 shipping protection)
- Subscription required to use Tool Drawer / third-party apps:
  - WiseOS Standalone (BYO carrier): **$14.99/mo**
  - Bundled carrier plans: Essential $24.99/mo (1GB) / Balanced $44.99/mo (5GB) / Unlimited $69.99/mo (≤50GB)

- Pricing model: hardware + mandatory monthly subscription (recurring, not one-shot)

## Mechanism / UM
- **Mechanism — how/why it works:** strip the OS down so the addictive surface area doesn't exist on the device. Hardware-level restriction (curated Tool Drawer) rather than software-overlay blocking (Bark / Screen Time). Combine with Family Portal parental oversight for the kid-buyer use case.
- **Problem-mechanism (causal story, shared):** smartphone = social media + browser + algorithmic feed = the source of distraction / addiction / harm to kids. Same causal story as Bark, Gabb, Pinwheel, Troomi, Light Phone — **SHARED across the entire kid-safe + minimalist-device set, NOT unique.**
- **UM:** **none.** Mechanism is "remove the bad apps at the OS level" — identical category move to Gabb, Pinwheel, Troomi, Light Phone. WiseOS is a feature-level differentiator (proprietary OS layer, ethical-design framing) but not a positioned-as-unique mechanism. Closest to a Feature-UM would be the **Family Portal + carrier bundling integration** but Bark Phone matches it.
