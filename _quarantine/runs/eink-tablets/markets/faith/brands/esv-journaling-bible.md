## Product

It is the category-defining journaling Bible: a complete redesign of Crossway's original (double-column) ESV Journaling Bible. Core product (Hardcover / TruTone / Cloth over Board) retail price **$44.99** on Crossway.org (current 2025 reissue, ISBN 979-8-8749-0034-2). ## Mechanism / UM
- mechanism(s) — how/why it works: Extra-wide (2-inch) ruled margins beside the biblical text give physical room to write directly next to specific verses; ruled lines keep handwriting aligned to the verse it comments on; single-column paragraph layout puts each verse on its own clean line so notes map one-to-one to text; smyth-sewn binding lets the book lie flat so writing is comfortable. The mechanism is purely physical-page-design — write your reflections in the same book, on the same page, as the verse.
- problem-mechanism (causal story, may be shared): N/A explicit. Implicit: notes/journals kept *separate* from the Bible become disconnected from the text — Crossway's framing solves "keep a journal... right inside the Bible," i.e. the problem of fragmented note-keeping. Not positioned as a uniquely diagnosed cause; it is a soft, shared assumption.
- UM: **none.** The wide-margin / ruled-margin mechanism is the category-defining standard of journaling Bibles, used identically by every competitor (NIV, CSB, ESV all ship wide-margin editions). It is a recognized-category feature, not a uniquely positioned mechanism. Crossway does not claim it as proprietary.
