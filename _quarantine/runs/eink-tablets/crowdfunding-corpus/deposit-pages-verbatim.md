# 3. Prelaunch.com platform template (canonical reservation page structure)

URL refs:
- help.prelaunch.com/en/articles/9514014-reservation-page-guidelines
- help.prelaunch.com/en/articles/8232392-how-does-the-reservation-funnel-work
- help.prelaunch.com/en/articles/8263057-what-is-a-reservation-deposit

What it is: Prelaunch.com is a major pre-launch platform (separate from LaunchBoom). They enforce a templated reservation page. Creators cannot edit the copy on the deposit page itself, only swap in the price numbers. This is the "lowest common denominator" reservation page format that most non-custom funnels use.

## Verbatim platform copy (the page Prelaunch.com renders for every creator)

### Required input fields (creator-side)
- "Deposit Price"
- "Regular Price"
- "Discounted Price"

### Platform-enforced behavior
"The offered discount will automatically be calculated once the 'Deposit Price', 'Regular Price' and 'Discounted Price' sections are filled."
"Users cannot add or remove any blocks on the reservation page, there's only one static block."
"At the moment it's not recommended to edit the text in the Paragraph section."

### Visitor-side flow copy (what the reservee sees)
**Reservation explainer (verbatim from Prelaunch.com Help Center):**
"This Reservation Deposit on Prelaunch.com is a binding agreement between you and each reservee, if you launch your product, they will be able to purchase it for the discounted price you offered them."

**On the reservation page itself:**
"On the reservation page, users encounter crucial information such as the product price, discounted price, and deposit amount."
"users are required to make a specific deposit to secure the product at the promised price, right after its official launch."

**Primary CTA (verbatim):** "Reserve my spot"
After clicking: user is redirected to the Stripe page for checkout.

**Secondary CTA (decliner path):** "No thanks"
Decliners are routed to a 5-question survey, then added to the Followers list.

**Post-payment flow:**
"will receive a confirmation email from Stripe with the subscription receipt"
"will automatically appear in your 'Followers list'"
"will be prompted to fill in the survey"

### Survey copy (Prelaunch.com decliner path)
"short 5-question survey. This will give you more understanding of what holds your potential user back before submitting their place with the reservation deposit."

### Deposit posture (binding agreement framing)
"Rights & Obligations" section header with three subsections:
- "Before launching the product on e-commerce or crowdfunding:"
- "After launching your product on e-commerce or crowdfunding:"
- "If you do not launch your product on e-commerce or crowdfunding:"
