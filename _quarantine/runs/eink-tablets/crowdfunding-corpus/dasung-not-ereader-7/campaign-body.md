## First-Screen Value Proposition (from press coverage)

Positioned as a 5-in-1 device:
1. E-reader
2. Android tablet
3. Video player
4. PC monitor (via mini-HDMI)
5. Mobile phone monitor (wireless secondary display)

> "It can be used as an ereader (despite the name suggesting the contrary), a tablet, a video player, a PC monitor, and a mobile phone monitor."
Source: liliputing.com

## About / Story Copy (reconstructed from press coverage)

Source: geeky-gadgets.com, 2018-10-26 (Julian Horsey)

Target audiences cited in campaign framing:
- "long-term mobile phone users experiencing eye discomfort"
- "children using tablets for cartoons or lessons"
- "mobile workers"
- "e-book readers seeking multifunctional e-ink devices"
Source: geeky-gadgets.com, 2018-10-26

Open Android platform pitch:
> "ran an open Android platform, allowing users to install standard applications like Twitter and Facebook"
