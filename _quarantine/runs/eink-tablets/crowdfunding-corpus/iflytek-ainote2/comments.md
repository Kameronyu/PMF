### Theme 2 — Handwriting Recognition Quality
Representative quote (eWritable, reviewer Dan, ~Nov 2025 review):

### Theme 3 — AI Features Feel Incomplete / Cloud-Dependent
Representative quote (eWritable review):
"AI features 'feel only half-realised' with slow response generation"
"Heavy reliance on cloud-based services requiring active WiFi"

### Theme 4 — Subscription Model Concern (Post-Launch)
From eReadersForum blog post ("iFLYTEK AINOTE 2 Subscription: What Changes, What Stays Free, and Why It Matters"):
The campaign did not prominently disclose a subscription model. A paid Pro tier emerged post-launch, gating "offline processing, expanded cloud capabilities, and access to newer AI models." This created concern among backers about what was included at purchase price.

### Theme 5 — China Data / Compliance Risk
Representative quote (e-inkreview.com, reviewer note):
"the company's terms require users to abide by export control laws adopted by China" — flagged as concern for GDPR and regulated data handling

### Theme 6 — Stylus Nib Quality
Representative quote (eWritable):
"The bundled stylus exhibits problematic nib flex causing 'uneven friction' and occasional squeaking. The reviewer notes substituting with alternative Wacom EMR pens immediately resolves writing inconsistencies."

### Theme 7 — No Front Light
Noted by multiple reviewers (eWritable, e-inkreview.com) as a design limitation. Not framed as a complaint per se but consistently raised as a missing feature vs. competing devices.
