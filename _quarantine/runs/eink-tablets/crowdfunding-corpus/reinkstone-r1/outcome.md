## Fulfillment Status

- "Shipped" classification (Kickstarter): Campaign page shows project as "Shipped"
- Reality: Partial fulfillment. First batch shipped December 2022 (14+ months late). Unknown percentage of 2,424 backers received units.
- Evidence of non-delivery: 1,318 comments on campaign, overwhelming majority are refund demands (per comments.md). Many backers explicitly state "never received."
- Last update: August 22, 2023. After this, no contact from creator.
- Total Kickstarter updates: 29 (per header) / 31 (per footer count discrepancy)

## Company Pivot Evidence

Reinkstone pivoted away from e-ink tablets entirely:

1. **Website (reinkstone.com, checked 2026-05-24):** No R1 tablet listed. No mention of R1 on homepage or collections. R1 product page URL (reinkstone.com/products/reinkstone-r1-with-stylus-case) returns 404. R1 with stylus URL (reinkstone.com/products/reinkstone-r1) also 404.

2. **Current product line (as of 2026-05-24):** Exclusively e-ink phone cases:
   - Reink Case C1 (iPhone, launched ~2023)
   - Reink Case C2 (iPhone 16 Pro)
   - Reink Case X1 (iPhone 16 Pro Max)
   - Reink Case Knight (Samsung Galaxy S24 Ultra)
   - Reink Case C3 (iPhone 17 series)
   - Reink Tag (Smart E-ink MFi Locator)

3. **Company self-description (current website):** "The pioneer of color e-ink screen phone cases" — no reference to tablets, no reference to R1.

4. **Timeline of pivot:** Reink Case C1 launched on Amazon for iPhone 14 series at $69.99 per ereaderpro.co.uk (dated "iPhone14 Series" = late 2022 release). Company was selling phone cases while simultaneously posting monthly R1 updates through Aug 2023.

5. **Kickstarter product page listing on reinkstone.com:** Search result found "Reinkstone R1 with Stylus & Case" at reinkstone.com/products/reinkstone-r1-with-stylus-case — returns 404 as of 2026-05-24 fetch.

## Backer Outcome Summary

- ~$1M+ raised from 2,424 backers
- Some units shipped (Dec 2022 "first batch"); exact count unknown
- Large number of backers never received device or refund
- No refunds issued per Trustpilot and comment evidence
- Company stopped communicating August 2023
- Creator's current business (Reink Case) shows no acknowledgment of R1 obligations
