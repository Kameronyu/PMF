## Post-Campaign Press

**Tom's Hardware — April 4, 2026**
Headline: "Hands-on with Modos Tech 13.3-inch e-paper monitors — we tried the current Dev Kit model and the next-gen Modos Flow touch"
Author: Mark Tyson
URL: https://www.tomshardware.com/monitors/portable-monitors/hands-on-with-modos-tech-13-3-inch-e-paper-monitors
Key quotes:
- "Eyestrain, discomfort, and exhaustion" motivated founders Alexander Soto and Wenting Zhang to establish Modos Tech in early 2022.
- Displays excel in "sunlight readability, reduced eye strain, and low energy consumption."

- Modos Tech aims to "invigorate and democratize the use of e-paper displays in computing."
Note: Hands-on with shipped unit AND next-gen Modos Flow; confirms product shipped and follow-on product (Flow) exists.
