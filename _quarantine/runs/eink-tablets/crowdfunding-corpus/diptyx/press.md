## Press Tone Summary

- **Maker/open-source press (CNX, Linuxiac, Hackaday, Hackster, Electronics-Lab):** Technical, positive, "finally" tone. Focus on ESP32 + open firmware + book-fold form factor.
- **E-reader press (Liliputing, Good e-Reader, NotebookCheck):** Positive, positioning as a niche alternative to Kindle/Kobo.
- **Design press (Yanko Design, TechEBlog):** Aesthetic-focused, "book-shaped reading object restored" framing.
- **Mainstream tech (Engadget, TechCrunch, The Verge, WIRED):** No coverage found.
- Distribution: Heavy maker/open-source press, zero mainstream lifestyle/consumer tech press.
