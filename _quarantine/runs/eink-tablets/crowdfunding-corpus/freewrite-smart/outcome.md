## DELAY ANALYSIS

- Promised: September 2015
- Actual backer delivery: March–May 2016
- Delay: approximately 6–8 months
- Delay framing: "eight-plus months late" (fordsbasement.com; consistent with timeline)
  Source: https://fordsbasement.com/hemingwrite-to-freewrite/

- Reasons cited in press:
  - "There's probably four or five companies just on the keyboard." (supply chain complexity)
  - "By far the two most expensive components for us are the keyboard and the screen."
  — Adam Leeb, Publishing Perspectives Feb 2016
  URL: https://publishingperspectives.com/2016/02/comes-tuesday-for-1p-embargo-freewrite-at-last-hits-the-market/
  - E-Ink screen: single-source supplier (implied constraint)

Company still operating as of 2025 (Wordrunner keyboard announced).
Source: Wikipedia / Astrohaus

Mixed reviews post-ship:
- Praise: aluminum construction, mechanical keyboard quality
