## Theme 3: Battery Life — Claimed vs. Delivered

Signal strength: HIGH

Claimed (NotebookCheck pre-campaign article, 2019-02-26):

"Eewrite E-Pad Is An E-Ink Android Tablet That Can Run A Whole Week Between Charges"
