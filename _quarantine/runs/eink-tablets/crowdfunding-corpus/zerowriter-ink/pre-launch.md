## Original DIY Zerowriter (Pre-Commercial Pre-Launch Signal)

The commercial Zerowriter Ink was preceded by a viral DIY project:
- **Hackaday.io project:** https://hackaday.io/project/193902-zerowriter
  - Created: December 3, 2023
  - Last updated: ~2024
  - Description: "Open e-ink typewriter designed as an entry level project to get people into hardware, raspberry pi, 3D printing, and DIY."
  - Cost: ~$50 minimal / under $200 full build
  - Community: r/zerowriter subreddit
- This existing DIY audience provided a built-in pre-launch warm list for the commercial Ink campaign.
- GitHub: https://github.com/zerowriter/zerowriter1 (original DIY)
- GitHub: https://github.com/zerowriter/zerowriter_ink (commercial Ink firmware)
