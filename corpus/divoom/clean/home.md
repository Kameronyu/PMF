        Questions Before You Order?

       Questions Before You Order?

       Find the right Divoom product and get clear answers about shipping, returns, warranty, and support.

       What makes Divoom different from ordinary speakers and displays?

       Divoom combines digital art, playful design, and creative hardware to make everyday spaces feel more personal. From retro pixel speakers and smart desk displays to digital photo frames and LED backpacks, each product is designed to add sound, visuals, or self-expression to your desk, room, gaming setup, or gift moment.

       Which Divoom product should I choose?

       Start with how you plan to use it. For a retro desk speaker or creative gift, explore MiniToo, Ditoo-Pro, and Tiivoo-2. For gaming, streaming, or pixel art displays, consider Pixoo64 II and Times Gate. For photos, memories, and personal desk displays, explore Times Frame. For portable karaoke and playful gifts, browse our karaoke speakers.

       Can I customize Divoom products with the Divoom app?

       Many Divoom speakers and pixel displays support app-based customization, including pixel art, animations, clocks, text, and display modes. Features vary by product, so please check the individual product page for the exact functions supported by your device.

       Do you offer free shipping?

       Free Standard Shipping is available to most major countries. Express Shipping is also available for $15.99 in eligible regions. Enter your shipping address at checkout to view the delivery methods available for your destination.

       How can I track my order?

       Once your order ships, you will receive a tracking email. Tracking details may take up to 3 business days to update after the shipping label is created.

       Can I return my order?

       Unused and undamaged products purchased directly from Divoom may be returned within 15 days of delivery. Products must include the original packaging and accessories. Please contact our support team before returning an item so we can provide the correct instructions.

       Does Divoom provide a product warranty?

       Yes. Products purchased directly from Divoom include a 12-month warranty for quality-related defects. If you experience an issue, contact our support team with your order details and proof of purchase so we can help review your case.
