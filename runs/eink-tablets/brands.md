## Notes

- **Tier C is not E Ink** but competes directly in the paper-feel / eye-care / distraction-free market. Daylight in particular markets straight at the optimization/biohacker niche — likely the most on-target competitor for the sketched space. Excluding Tier C drops it.
- Tier B regional brands (iReader, Hanvon, Crema, Ridibooks, Guoyue, Pubu) have no Western Meta ads and often no English landing page — analyzer Ads Library + landing-page steps will return empty for them; product-page extraction still works.
- ememo (2.7" sticky note) and Modos (monitor/dev-kit) are edge cases — barely "tablets."
