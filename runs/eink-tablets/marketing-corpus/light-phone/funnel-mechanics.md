## Spec sheet (verbatim, Light-supplied through launch press)

### Light Phone III specs (consolidated from Yahoo/Tech, Engadget, TechCrunch, Slate, US Mobile — all Light-supplied)
- "3.92-inch AMOLED monochrome display" / "a sleek black-and-white OLED display"
- "matte glass that should make the Light Phone 3 easy on the eyes"
- Qualcomm SM4450 chipset
- 128GB storage, 6GB RAM
- 50MP rear camera (defaults to 12MP), 8MP front camera
- 1,800 mAh battery, user-replaceable
- "a camera and NFC chip for future payment tools"
- "two-step shutter button with center focus and a fixed focal length"
- "fingerprint ID" (on power button)
- USB-C, GPS, Bluetooth 5.0, eSIM support
- 5G sub-6 support
- IP54 (water/dust)
- "improves repairability, as you can now access the battery, and replace the screen and USB port"
- "3.5mm headphone jack" (US Mobile)
- "Phone, Alarm, Directions, Music, Notes, Calendar" — confirmed tool list (Digital Trends launch coverage)
- Extended tool list (per Light's launch statement to Yahoo/Tech): "alarm, a timer, a calculator, a calendar, a directory, directions, notes/voice memo, and a simple music or podcast player"
- Explicit exclusions (Light-supplied, multiple sources): "No Social Media Apps (Instagram, TikTok, X), No Email or Browser, No App Store or third-party apps, No push notifications"
