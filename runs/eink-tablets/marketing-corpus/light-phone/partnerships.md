## Major partnership: Noble Mobile × Light (May 2026)

**Source:** TechCrunch, May 19 2026 — "The minimalist Light Phone teams up with Andrew Yang's Noble Mobile, which pays you to stop doomscrolling"
URL: https://techcrunch.com/2026/05/19/the-minimalist-light-phone-teams-up-with-andrew-yangs-noble-mobile-which-pays-you-to-stop-doomscrolling/

Direct quotes (verbatim):

**Joe Hollier (Light co-founder):**
- "The Light Phone is designed to be used as little as possible"
- "I think what's exciting about the Noble launch is not just that the barrier to entry is lower"
- "We just tried to design our camera by taking out what we felt like was the culprit of people actually falling out of the moment, which is sharing"

**Kaiwei Tang (Light co-founder):**
- "I took 27,000 iPhone photos last year, and I've looked at them zero times"
- "It's not about asking people to [either] give up their technology, or use this AI 6G smartphone"

- Light Phone III: $699 (standard purchase, current as of May 2026)
- Noble Mobile plan: $50/month, 2-year contract = $1,200 total
- Data allowance: 5 GB included
- Cashback: Up to $5 per unused GB (Light Phone plan specific)
- Availability: 500 units immediately through Noble Mobile on Tuesday (May 19, 2026)
