Mudita Kompakt
Mudita Kompakt
More offline. More life.

A minimalist E Ink® phone for less screen time, and fewer distractions. Essential features with clear UI and a privacy focus.

Meet Kompakt

Eliminate distractions

Stay connected while being present. With only the essentials and a clean, distraction-free interface, powered by a custom operating system, Mudita Kompakt helps you focus on what truly matters without unnecessary interruptions or clutter.

Enhance your privacy

Your data stays yours. Mudita Kompakt ensures no tracking, no ads, and no distracting apps. It features a dedicated button for a hardware-level cutoff of microphones and the GSM modem, while also disabling the camera, WiFi, and Bluetooth at the software level, giving you privacy and peace of mind whenever you need it.

Comfort for your eyes

The 4.3" E Ink® screen delivers a paper-like reading experience, reducing eye strain and eliminating blue light. It remains clear and readable even in bright sunlight, perfect for those who want to stay connected without sacrificing their eye health.

Life with ease

Built to endure daily life, Mudita Kompakt combines up to 6 days of battery life, offline maps for worry-free navigation, a 3.5mm headphone jack for reliable audio, a durable ergonomic design, and a camera to digitize documents or capture memories.

Recognition for Intentional Design

The minimalist design of Mudita Kompakt has been formally recognized with a Reddot Winner 2025 award and an iF Design Award 2025. This validation from two of the world's most respected design institutions affirms our core belief that technology should be simple, intentional, and put user well-being first. The product was also noted as a Top Pick 2025 by TechAeris for its contribution to mindful technology.

Connectivity That Fits Your Needs

Mudita Kompakt comes in two versions optimized for specific regions: Global and North American. To ensure the best performance, the correct version will be automatically selected for you at checkout based on your shipping country. If you require a specific version for a destination outside its standard region (e.g., the North American version shipped to Europe), please contact our Customer Support team before placing your order.

Global Optimized

This version holds certifications required in selected markets, including Europe and the United States (e.g., FCC and PTCRB). This makes it also easier to stay connected while traveling across North America, Asia, or South America.

North America Optimized

Tailored for consistent and dependable connectivity in the US and Canada (FCC and PTCRB certified), where mobile networks have unique requirements compared to the rest of the globe. This version will still work in other parts of the world, though not as reliably as in North America.
