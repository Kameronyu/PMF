## Verdict

SHIPPED — but significantly late. ~2-year delay from original June 2019 estimate.
Not vaporware. Product eventually fully fulfilled (no evidence of partial-ship or abandonment).

## Wikipedia Shipping Note Discrepancy

Wikipedia (Astrohaus article) states: "first units shipped January 2019."
This contradicts multiple 2020-dated backer reviews describing "2-year waits."
Reconciliation: January 2019 likely refers to a handful of early test/developer units or
is an error. Mass backer fulfillment clearly occurred ~Oct–Nov 2020.

---

## Post-Ship Retail Status

Product went to retail after campaign fulfillment.
Retail price: $599 (original), later $549 (getfreewrite.com product page 2026: $549)
Good e-Reader listing price: $769 (inflated third-party)
Ghost Edition (transparent): launched November 2023 as limited release
Source: en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Astrohaus

## Overall Pattern

This is NOT a vaporware case. It is a "hardware is hard + COVID delay" pattern:
- Overoptimistic original timeline (June 2019)
- Multiple revised dates, each missed
- ~2-year total delay from original estimate
- Quality product eventually shipped
- No evidence of refund disputes post-fulfillment at scale
- Product remains in retail as of 2026

Comparable to EeWrite E-Pad delay pattern, but with better outcome (no reported quality
degradation of the magnitude EeWrite had; main complaint was e-ink latency, not bricking).
