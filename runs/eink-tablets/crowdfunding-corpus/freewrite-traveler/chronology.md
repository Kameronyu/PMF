## Pacing Narrative

- Day 1: Extraordinary velocity — 28-minute goal completion.
- Week 1: $300k+ (= 600% of goal in 7 days). This is a top-1% IGG pacing.
- Day 30: $530k+ — roughly flat/linear mid-campaign growth after initial burst.
  Adam Leeb's note: "We beat that [$500k estimate] and there is plenty of time left!"
- Final: ~$600k IGG close + ~$145k post-campaign = ~$745k total.

Mid-campaign flatness relative to initial burst is typical for high-pacing campaigns —
the day-1 surge carries most of the total raise, with steady but slower daily pledges in
the middle weeks, and a final-week bump from urgency messaging (standard pattern).
