## Delay Cause
1. Manufacturing ramp with Soldered Electronics in Croatia
2. ~300 faulty spacebars from original production batch — required replacement parts
3. US tariff complications (15% tariff on Croatian goods) requiring backer communications
4. Likely supply chain / production scheduling issues not publicly detailed during the 9-month pre-ship silence

## Ink vs Fold Clarification
- Zerowriter Ink: Crowd Supply, $143,023 raised, 570 backers, 2024
- Zerowriter Fold: Kickstarter, separate campaign, ~$239-258 campaign price, funded in 42 minutes, 2025 launch, estimated ship late 2026
- The $143,023 / 476% / 570 backers figures are EXCLUSIVELY for the Ink (Crowd Supply). The Fold is a separate Kickstarter campaign with different metrics.
- PCWorld (May 19, 2026): "Adam's first crowdfunding campaign for a writer gadget design raised over $100,000 to produce and send to backers" — referring to Zerowriter Ink.
- Press conflation risk: Any article citing "$143k" or "476%" for "Zerowriter" is citing Ink numbers. Fold metrics are different and not yet public at capture date.
