## Post-Campaign Product Fate

- Dasung launched Not-eReader 103 (10.3") on Kickstarter, Sep 2020
  - $25,954 raised / $500 goal / 5,191% funded / 38 backers
  - Avg pledge: ~$683 USD (Wacom model at $838)
  - Shipped per Good e-Reader review (small pool)
  - Source: goodereader.com; campaign-roster.md note
- Not-eReader 133 (13.3") was eventually released as retail product
  - Good e-Reader review exists: https://goodereader.com/blog/tablet-slates/dasung-not-an-e-reader-133-monitor-tablet-combo-device-a-review
- Dasung continues to sell Paperlike monitors and the Link (IGG 2022)
  - They have not returned to broad consumer crowdfunding since the 7.8" debacle

## Failure Pattern Classification

- **Type:** Campaign outran production capacity
- **Root cause signals:** First crowdfunding attempt; hardware company without consumer manufacturing experience; over-promised ship date (Mar 2019 from Oct 2018 launch is aggressive for custom hardware); went silent when production problems hit
- **Community management:** Near-zero — no updates for 2+ months, distributors not briefed, company stopped responding to emails post-ship
- **Launch of new campaign while old backers unfulfilled:** YES — Sep 2020 KS while Jul 2020 still <60 of "hundreds" fulfilled (classic bad-faith signal to community)
