## CAVEAT ON "~$100k+, 450+ backers, 350%+" PRESS NUMBERS

Several search snippets and the PCWorld article reference "over $100,000 raised" in the context
of the Zerowriter Fold Kickstarter campaign. This appears to be a conflation error:

- **Zerowriter Ink (Crowd Supply, 2024):** $143,530 USD, 574 backers — this is the "$100k+" figure
- **Zerowriter Fold (KS, 2026):** $204,626 CAD (~$149k USD) as of day 5, 558 backers, 292% funded

The PCWorld article (May 19, 2026) phrase "with over $100,000 raised to produce and send it out
to your backers" is explicitly referring to the PRIOR Zerowriter Ink campaign, not the Fold.
The "350%+" and "funded in 42 min" numbers circulating in press snippets refer to the Fold
but may be mixed with Ink raise figures in aggregated summaries.

Authoritative Fold-specific data as of day 5: $204,626 CAD raised, 292%, 558 backers, 41 min to fund.
