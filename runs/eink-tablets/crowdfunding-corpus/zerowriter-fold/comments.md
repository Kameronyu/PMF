## Proxy Signal: Backer Concerns (from press/interview, not KS comments)

The following themes are documented in the public record and likely reflect
backer concern categories, though they are NOT verbatim KS comments:

### Theme 1 — Shipping delay precedent (from PCWorld interview)
Creator acknowledged Zerowriter Ink took "about a year longer to finish than I had expected."
Potential objection: fulfillment risk given prior late delivery.

### Theme 2 — Tariff / import costs
From creator LP: "US customers: Approximately 10% tariff on Canadian imports (as of 2026)"
Potential objection: total cost higher than headline price.

### Theme 3 — Battery range ambiguity
From creator LP FAQ: battery range 50–100 hours "depends on writing pace, thinking time,
screen refresh settings, and brightness level."
Potential objection: overstated battery headline.

### Theme 4 — Software maturity
From PCWorld interview: "I started with that kind of basic setup, and I started adding things..."
Creator explicitly excluded spell check, grammar check, autocomplete from scope.
Potential objection: limited word processor feature set vs. cost.

### Theme 5 — Hinge durability
Not explicitly flagged in any captured press or comments. Gadgective note: "the Zerowriter Fold
is simply a clamshell device and has a 6″ e-paper display that does not fold" — suggests the
display does NOT fold (only the keyboard/enclosure hinges). Durability of hinge unclear.
