## FAQ (verbatim from creator LP)

**Battery Variation (50-100 hours):**
"Depends on writing pace, thinking time, screen refresh settings, and brightness level. Slow writers with screen light off see longer battery life than those using frontlight continuously."

**Compatibility:**
"Works with Mac, PC, and Linux via plain text files. No proprietary software or drivers required."

**If You Sign Up But Don't Order:**

## Creator Bio / About Team

**Founder:** Adam (Adam Wilk)
**Company:** Zerowriter (The Zerowriter Company)
**Location:** Ottawa, Ontario, Canada
**Founded:** 2024 (DIY Raspberry Pi project that went commercial)

From PCWorld interview (Adam Wilk, May 19, 2026):

"I started a business in my 20s where I made handmade leather journals. That was kind of my first taste of entrepreneurship."

"When COVID came around, I suddenly had a lot more time on my hands... I took an e-ink display from China and a Raspberry Pi keyboard, put it all together, and added a nice case to it. I made that project and it got really popular. I put it up on Reddit, and a bunch of news aggregators picked it up."

"I've worked with Soldered in Croatia primarily for this [Ink]... With the Fold, I am looking to switch it up a bit... I want to have a little more control with assembly... last leg assembly in Canada."

**Team description from creator LP:**
"a small team that builds great tools" with contractors and developers globally (Canada, Spain, Croatia, Singapore, and beyond)

**Production capacity claim:**
"Exceeds 300 units monthly, all hand-assembled and personally tested in-house."

Source: zerowriter.ink/pages/zerowriter-fold; PCWorld (Michael Crider, 2026-05-19)
