## Story / About Copy

### Product lineup described in press release (verbatim from GlobeNewswire / Yahoo Finance, 2024-08-08):

"The Aipaper from Viwoods is a series of e-notes comprising two tablet devices – the Aipaper with a 10.65-inch display and the Aipaper Mini that comes with an 8.2-inch e-paper display."

"The 10.65-inch display is based on the latest E Ink Carta 1300 display and uses a flexible screen which is more resilient against scratches and impacts."

"Both [models] feature 300 PPI resolution and include styluses with dual tip options."

### AI capabilities (verbatim from press/Good e-Reader, 2024-08-08):

"Aipaper's powerful AI can analyze content, organize handwritten notes, assist with research and writing, provide extensive AI Q&A, and support handwriting and voice input."

"[the device includes] Handwriting-to-text conversion capabilities with real-time, selective, and full-page recognition options"
— source: KS FAQ via WebSearch snippet 2026-05-24

### Features (from press release / web3pr Medium, 2024-08-08):

"Document templates (SWOT, STAR, Quadrant, 5W1H)"
"Meeting minute generation"
"Document management with passcode protection"
"File transfer via QR code, Bluetooth, email, or ViTransfer app"
"Local-only storage (24-hour web backup auto-deletion)"
"Handwriting-to-text conversion (three methods)"
"Bluetooth keyboard connectivity"
"No video/audio playback" [distraction-free by design]
"24-hour automatic document cleanup on web platform"
"No cloud services; local privacy protection"

## FAQ (from KS FAQ page via WebSearch snippet, 2026-05-24)

Source: https://www.kickstarter.com/projects/viwoods/viwoods-aipaper-e-ink-tablet/faqs (403 to direct fetch; below from search snippet extraction)

**Q: Warranty coverage?**
A: "14-month warranty that covers the main device and the writing stylus for functional defects. The warranty does not apply to accessories such as protective cases or pen tips, and it does not cover damage from accidents, unauthorized modifications, or the use of non-official accessories."

**Q: Shipping times?**
A: "US customers: 3-7 days to receive; Other countries: 8-15 days to receive. Additionally, non-US orders ship via international express, duties borne by the customer."

**Q: Battery life?**
A: "up to one week of daily use (2 hours per day) or 4 weeks on standby"

**Q: Handwriting-to-text?**
A: "AiPaper supports handwriting-to-text conversion through three distinct ways: real-time as you write, selective conversion of any portion of the document, and full-page AI-powered recognition for unmatched accuracy."

**Q: Shipping regions?**
A: "Asia, Europe, North America, and Oceania" (from search snippet)
