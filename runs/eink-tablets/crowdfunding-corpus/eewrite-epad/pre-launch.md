## Summary

Pre-launch evidence is thin. The earliest press coverage is from NotebookCheck (2019-02-26) and The eBook Reader (2019-03-02), both citing spec sheets. No dedicated pre-launch LP or mailing list found. GoodEReader published early bullish coverage ("might be the best e-reader ever made") in the days around launch. The PR Newswire release on launch day is the clearest evidence of intentional launch marketing. Community skepticism about the brand's legitimacy was already present on MobileRead 3 weeks before launch.
