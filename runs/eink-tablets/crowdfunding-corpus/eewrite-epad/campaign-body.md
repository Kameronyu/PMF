## Full specifications as claimed in campaign / marketing materials

(Compiled from press release + pre-launch articles — all sourced 2019-02-26 to 2019-04-28)

- Display: 10.3" E Ink Mobius display, 1872 x 1404 resolution, 227 PPI
- Display technology: "plastic based TFT that is flexible, lightweight and durable" (PR Newswire)
- Processor: MediaTek Helio X20 MT6797 (deca-core / 10-core)
- RAM: 2GB (stretch goal: 4GB)
- Storage: 32GB (stretch goal: 64GB); microSD expansion
- Battery: 4,100 mAh
- Battery life claimed: "one week to an entire month" (NotebookCheck pre-launch)
- Connectivity: Wi-Fi, Bluetooth, 4G LTE with SIM card slot, GPS
- Input: WACOM stylus (4096 pressure levels), capacitive touchscreen
- OS: Android 8.0 with Google Play Store pre-loaded
- Audio: stereo speakers, microphone
- Port: USB-C
- Weight/dimensions: 392g, 6.8mm thick (from NotebookCheck review)
- Front-light: yes, dual color temperatures
