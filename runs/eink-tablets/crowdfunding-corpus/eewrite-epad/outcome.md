# outcome.md — EeWrite E-Pad (eewrite-epad)
Captured: 2026-05-24

---

## Ship status: SHIPPED — BUT BROKEN PROMISE

Category: Not vaporware. Not late (or only moderately late). Shipped with critical quality failures.

---

## What was promised vs. what shipped

| Claim | Delivered |
|---|---|
| "battery life between one week to an entire month" | Charged every 2 days; 35% drain in 4 hours under review |
| Smooth Android experience with 10-core processor | "snail's pace" navigation; "5 frames per second" browsing |
| 1-week+ battery life (headline on CoolThings, GoodEReader) | ~2-day battery; 5-hour charge time from 1% |
| Android OS polished for E-Ink | "big and pixelated," phone OS blown up, not tablet-optimized |
| WACOM stylus note-taking | "noticeable delay from when you start drawing to when you complete it" |
| Ships August 2019 (KS) | Some units shipped by ~June–September 2019; processor redesign delayed others |

---

## Quality failure evidence (verbatim)

NotebookCheck review (verbatim):
"response times are so poor that basic functions like navigation, touch-typing or writing... move unreliably and at a snail's pace"
"E-Ink screen is abysmally slow to the point of frustration"
"Browsing at 5 frames per second" (subheadline)
"hard for us to recommend paying the high $400+ USD price premium"
Source: https://www.notebookcheck.net/EeWrite-E-Pad-E-Ink-Tablet-Review.415145.0.html

GoodEReader "going back to drawing board" (verbatim):
"the deca-core Helio X20 processor drained the battery so fast, that it needed to be recharged every two days"
"the 10.3 device employing the Android phone OS, which made everything big and pixelated"
"a prototype in their review cannot be upgraded and is 'basically a doorstop at this point'"
Source: https://goodereader.com/blog/electronic-readers/wisky-is-going-back-to-the-drawing-board-with-the-eewrite-e-pad

GoodEReader review (verbatim):
"The Wisky has some serious bugs in order to be ready for prime time"
Rating: 3.0/5
Source: https://goodereader.com/blog/reviews/wisky-ee-write-e-pad-review

HLarch backer review, NotebookChat forum (2019-09-29, verbatim):
"The O/S is so basic, it allows only very few customizations"
"The reader is really, really basic — no highlighting or note-taking features"
"Software described as 'not ready for prime-time'"
"pre-production hardware costing ~$600"
Source: https://www.notebookchat.com/index.php?topic=95845.0

## Redesign / drawing-board event

After early units shipped and were reviewed poorly, Wisky announced:
- Processor downgrade: deca-core → octa-core (to fix battery drain)
- OS and UI overhaul (to fix pixelation/clunkiness)
- No firm new ship date given ("sometime in the next few months")
Source: https://goodereader.com/blog/electronic-readers/wisky-is-going-back-to-the-drawing-board-with-the-eewrite-e-pad

## Overall outcome classification

SHIPPED BROKEN PROMISE — device was delivered but core value propositions (week-long battery, smooth Android navigation) failed. Creator went back to drawing board mid-campaign. High pledge cancellation rate during campaign. Reviewed devices rated 3/5 (GoodEReader), called not worth $400+ (NotebookCheck). Followup product shifted to LCD hybrid.
