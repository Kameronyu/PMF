## Notes on Data Conflicts

The Linuxiac Dec 14 article reports "$17,410 raised, 39 days remaining" — significantly lower than the Dec 12 CNX/Liliputing figure of $64,440. Possible explanations:
1. Linuxiac pulled from a cached or pre-surge Crowd Supply page state
2. Linuxiac's $17,410 represents a different metric (e.g., Crowd Supply's own tracking vs. cumulative pledges)
3. Reporting error

The Dec 12 CNX/Liliputing figure ($64,440) is corroborated by the evolution record and is the more credible number for launch-day raise. Final figure ($86,610 / 240 backers) confirmed by Crowd Supply search aggregate.
