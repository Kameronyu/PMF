### Design philosophy statement
"The Hemingwrite is a minimalist digital typewriter for distraction free writing.
It marries the simplicity of writing on a vintage typewriter or word processor with
the conveniences of modern technology."
Source: Core77 Design Awards, 2015
URL: https://designawards.core77.com/Consumer-Product/31688/Hemingwrite

"The purpose of the Hemingwrite is to aid professional and aspiring writers during

that would be a hindrance."
Source: Core77 Design Awards, 2015

"The Hemingwrite is designed to be ultra simple and straightforward. Where other

"Friction is the Enemy of Flow — The design of the Hemingwrite is entirely focused

### Origin story (Adam Leeb, from creator blog)
"In that very first conversation, we had established the vision for the eventual
product. It would have a mechanical keyboard, E Ink screen, and would save all your
drafts seamlessly to the cloud with Wi-Fi."
— Adam Leeb
Source: adamleeb.com, "The Freewrite is not a typewriter — Creating Freewrite, Part 1"
URL: https://adamleeb.com/the-freewrite-is-not-a-typewriter-creating-freewrite-part-1/

"The origin had nothing to do with typewriters. In no way did we want to create a
modern typewriter or recreate the typewriter. Typewriters are terrible!"
— Adam Leeb
Source: adamleeb.com, Part 1

"It's like living your whole life trying to set nails with a log and then someone
handing you a hammer."
— Adam Leeb, on discovering the draft-first methodology
Source: adamleeb.com, Part 1

## TECHNICAL SPECS (from campaign + press)

- ~6" E-Paper display (e-ink)
- Full-size mechanical keyboard with Cherry MX Brown switches
- Durable, lightweight aluminum housing
- Approximately 4 lbs
- 8"L x 10"W x 2.5"H
- 4+ week battery life
- 1 million+ page memory
- Cloud sync: Google Drive, Dropbox, Evernote, iCloud, OneDrive, SpiderOak

Source: Core77 Design Awards, TechCrunch Dec 10 2014, Engadget Nov 2014
