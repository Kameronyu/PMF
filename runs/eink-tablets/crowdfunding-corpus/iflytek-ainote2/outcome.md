## Fulfillment Anomaly
One documented issue (not mass complaint):
"In the Kickstarter scenario, an early backer received the device weeks after some later backers and after the device was already available on Amazon."
Source: eReadersForum.com aggregator

This suggests KS fulfillment queue ran behind Amazon/retail channel briefly — device available on Amazon before all KS backers received theirs. Not uncommon for large retail brands using KS as a pre-order vehicle.

## Post-Launch Software / Subscription Controversy
- iFLYTEK introduced a paid Pro subscription tier post-launch
- Free tier retains: voice transcription (unlimited minutes), handwriting conversion, multi-language transcription, translation, speaker labeling
- Pro tier gates: offline processing, expanded cloud, newer AI models
- Source: eReadersForum blog "iFLYTEK AINOTE 2 Subscription: What Changes, What Stays Free, and Why It Matters"
