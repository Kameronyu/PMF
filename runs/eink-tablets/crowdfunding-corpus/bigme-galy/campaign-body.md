## Specs as Quoted in Campaign / Press

- 8" E Ink Gallery 3 color display, 1920×1440, 300 ppi (color and B/W modes both at 300 ppi)
- Four refresh modes: Extreme Speed (B/W, 350 ms), Fast Color / Speed Mode (500 ms), Standard (750–1000 ms), HD / Best Color (1500 ms)
- Four-particle ACeP ink: cyan / magenta / yellow / white (no color filter array)
- ~50,000 colors
- ARM Cortex A55 octa-core 2.3 GHz / 6 GB RAM / 128 GB storage + microSD (up to 512 GB–1 TB depending on source)
- 3,000–4,000 mAh battery (sources vary; 3,000 mAh cited by Liliputing/Notebookcheck; 4,000 mAh in evolution record and Good e-Reader pre-launch)
- Wacom EMR stylus, 4096 pressure levels, laser pointer, voice-control button, magnetic side-charging
- Front light, adjustable color temperature, 36-step brightness
- Dual stereo speakers, 4 noise-cancelling mics
- 8 MP rear + 5 MP front cameras (some pre-launch sources say 5 MP + 5 MP; Nov 16 launch confirmed 8 MP rear)
- Android 11 + Google Play preinstalled
- Fingerprint sensor
- 320 g, 7 mm thin, aluminum-alloy chassis
- BT 5.1, Wi-Fi 2.4/5 GHz, USB-C
- Formats: TXT, ePUB, PDF, FB2, MOBI, RTF, CBZ, CBR
