## Product
- **ememo電子便條紙 (ememo electronic memo / sticky note)** — single product line. A 2.7" E Ink electrophoretic display in a natural-bamboo body. Magnetic mount (stick it on a fridge, wall, desk). WiFi-connected; content pushed/updated via a companion app (PWA web app). Rechargeable battery, 5V (USB) charging. Touch input on the device.
- Functions: e-paper message board (push text/notes remotely), image transfer (display photos/pictures), to-do list page, weather display, stock-quote display. Positioned as fixed-mount OR portable.
- Crowdfunding bundle/price tiers (zeczec, NT$):
  - Classic Single Set (經典單人組) — 1 device + 1 screen. Early-bird ~NT$2,400-implied lower; stated future/retail price NT$2,400.
  - Couple Set (雙人組) — 2 devices + 2 screens. ~NT$1,600/set avg early-bird; future price NT$4,800.
  - Group / multi-person Set — 5 devices + 5 screens. ~NT$1,580/set avg early-bird; future price NT$12,000.
  - Add-on screens: 2-screen pack future NT$1,440 (~NT$525/screen avg early-bird); 4-screen pack future NT$2,880 (~NT$500/screen avg).
- Note: the "screen" appears to be a swappable/extra e-paper display element sold alongside the base device — the bundles pair devices with screens 1:1 and offer screens as standalone add-ons. Exact device/screen relationship not fully confirmed (zeczec page inaccessible).
- Format: low-cost connected e-ink memo display. Roughly NT$1,580–2,400 (~US$50–80) per unit.

## Transformation(s)
- Verbatim (campaign tagline): "讓你的日常互動更有溫度" — "make your everyday interactions warmer / more human."
- Verbatim (descriptor): a device to "記錄生活大小事" — "record life's big and small things," and that "讓你的日常互動更有溫度."
- Your read: the marketed transformation is emotional / relational, not productivity or focus. ememo sells warmer everyday connection — leaving notes/messages/pictures for family or coworkers on a physical, always-visible e-paper board. Secondary transformation = lightweight organization (reminders, to-do, weather/stock at a glance). It is NOT pitched on eye-strain, distraction-free focus, digital minimalism, or paperless note-taking — the dominant transformations elsewhere in this e-ink set. This is the clearest reason it is an edge case.

- Bamboo material + "更有溫度" (warmth) framing skews toward design-conscious, gift-oriented Taiwanese consumers — the zeczec crowdfunding audience (design/lifestyle early adopters).
- Read: broad-ish lifestyle/home niche — households and small teams who want a charming connected message board. Not an identity community (no "students," "writers," "minimalists"). Closest label: design-conscious home/gift buyers. Narrow by geography (Taiwan only).

## Mechanism / UM
- mechanism(s) referenced: E Ink electrophoretic 2.7" display (always-on, low power, paper-like); natural bamboo casing; WiFi connectivity + companion PWA app for remote content push; magnetic mounting; on-device touch.
- UM type played: **Product UM** — the differentiator is the SOLUTION framed around a mechanism: "世界首創天然竹料電子顯示器" (world's first natural-bamboo e-paper display). The bamboo-wrapped e-paper IS the unique mechanism — it positions ememo as a warmer, more natural object than a plastic gadget or a glowing screen. No Problem UM (it does not argue a novel cause of a problem). A weak secondary Feature UM exists in the WiFi/app remote-push capability, but that is table-stakes for connected displays, not a headline differentiator. Headline UM = bamboo + e-paper.

## Sophistication
- market sophistication stage this brand plays at: **Stage 1–2.** ememo introduces what is effectively a new transformation/category for its audience — a bamboo-wrapped, app-connected e-paper message board sold on emotional warmth ("更有溫度"), not on e-ink's standard eye-comfort/focus benefits. For its Taiwan home/gift niche this is a genuinely new thing, so it behaves like Stage 1 (introduce a new transformation) with light Stage 2 scaffolding (a Product UM = bamboo e-paper, plus crowdfunding social-proof and a value/bundle ladder). It does NOT play the Stage 4 feature-spec war (panel generation, refresh rate, RAM) that the writing-tablet and e-reader brands in this set fight. Caveat: it competes in a near-empty category, so "low sophistication stage" reflects an immature/tiny market, not a strong position.
