## Transformation(s)
- dominant frame: **focused, distraction-free work — get more done by thinking better.** The product is sold as a tool that changes how you work and think, not just a notepad. Current site framing leads on "focused work" (a shift from the prior "for better thinking" / pure-cognition framing toward a work-productivity outcome).
- secondary: (1) **paperless / paper-into-digital-workflow** — eliminate desk clutter, organize and search thousands of handwritten pages digitally; (2) **reduced stress / screen-fatigue relief** — calm, eye-strain-free single-tasking device vs. a connected multitasking tablet.
- evidence: "reMarkable: The Paper Tablets for Focused Work" (homepage hero); "The paper tablets made for work" (ads); "a paper tablet made for thinking" (Paper Pure ad); Paper Pure page cites "35% less stress" and "20% more focus" vs. a PC; "Your place to think" (Paper Pure); "reMarkable Paper Pro: for a focused, organized, and paperless workplace" (ad); "Bring paper into your digital workflow" (Paper Pro subhead).

Creators (sketching, content ideation) addressed as a lighter secondary cluster, mostly via Paper Pro Move influencer ads.

## Mechanism / UM
- mechanism(s) — how/why it works: reflective e-ink panel (Canvas / Canvas Color) reflects ambient light instead of emitting it → no blue light, no flicker, no eye strain; sub-1mm pen-to-ink distance + textured/matte-glass surface → paper-like writing feel; deliberate single-purpose OS — no apps, no browser, no notifications → focus is enforced by design; MyScript-class handwriting conversion → handwritten pages become searchable/exportable digital text.
- problem-mechanism (causal story, may be shared): connected multitasking tablets and PCs pull you out of focused work — notifications, app-switching, and emitted-light screens cause distraction, stress, and eye strain. This is a shared industry causal story (every e-ink-focus competitor names it), so it is NOT a UM.
- UM: **Product-UM** — "a paper tablet: a dedicated, single-purpose, distraction-free space for focused work," positioned against multitasking LCD tablets. reMarkable originated this category-defining Product-UM. Secondary **Feature-UM** layered on: Canvas Color "millions of color ink particles, unlike other digital paper displays" + sub-1mm pen distance — feature-level differentiation within the category it owns. Note: the "paper tablet" Product-UM has largely gone universal (Boox, Supernote, Kindle Scribe, Kobo all play it), so its uniqueness is decaying; reMarkable now leans on brand + feature upgrades.
