## Mechanism / UM

- **mechanism(s) — how/why it works:** purpose-built hardware + locked OS + Gabb's own MVNO cellular service + proprietary apps (Messenger, Music, Maps) that replace consumer-internet equivalents. The combination of "no browser + no app store + can't-be-circumvented controls + curated whitelist" is the full mechanism. Software lock + hardware ownership + carrier integration = vertically integrated walled garden.
- **problem-mechanism (causal story):** "smartphones/social media are addictive and harmful to kids; parental controls bolted onto iPhones/Androids get bypassed; the only safe phone is one designed safe from the silicon up." This is a SHARED causal story across Gabb / Bark / Pinwheel / Troomi / Wait Until 8th — not unique to Gabb.
- **UM:** **Product-UM (honest assessment).** Gabb's "phone designed safe from scratch, not bolted-on parental controls" is genuinely distinctive within the *general* phone market — vs Apple Screen Time or Family Link as filter overlays, Gabb is a fundamentally different product. **However, within the kid-safe-phone category** (Pinwheel, Troomi, Bark Phone all play here), the walled-garden approach is the category convention, not unique. So:
  - vs general smartphone market: Product-UM. Verbatim positioning: "free from web browsers, open source app stores, and social media apps" + controls that "can't be removed or circumvented."
  - vs kid-safe-phone competitors: none / shared. The UM has decayed to category-table-stakes within its peer set; differentiation now happens on price + curation taste + content ecosystem (Gabb Music, etc.) + religious-app whitelist.
