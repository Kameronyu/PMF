  about" is the *desire underneath it*. They are one layered hook, not two angles. Gift framing rides as a

Adapts the blueprint's hero logic to a product that wins on aesthetics, not numbers. The spec-led doc's own
warning ("copying Omega's thinness is the main failure mode") is why the trust scaffold stays — and is heavier
here because the founder is the *only* trust asset.

## 5. Offer + urgency (LOCKED)

Mechanic = inkleaf's two-step deposit-to-reserve.

- **MSRP anchor (public, Mar 2027):** **$299** — must be *justified by rarity* (limited founder batch /
  hand-finished), NOT by specs (it can't beat a $199 Playdate on specs).
- **Founder price (Aug 2026, locked this batch):** **$146 — "51% off"** (mirrors inkleaf's SAVE-51% frame).
- **Refundable deposit (credited to founder price):** **$5** (locked — credited to the $146; ~3.4%, low friction).
- **Urgency:** time-gap (Aug 2026 vs Mar 2027) + batch price-permanence + cohort shipping priority.
  **No countdown timer, no fake unit-scarcity** (matches inkleaf; matches DR "fake scarcity is fatal").
