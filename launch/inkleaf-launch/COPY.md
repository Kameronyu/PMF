## Product spec (already on file)

Inkleaf is a foldable dual-screen e-paper notebook that opens into a two-page digital workspace and folds down into a compact notebook-sized device. Two 10.3-inch black-and-white E Ink screens, 4096-level pressure-sensitive stylus with eraser. Read on one screen while writing on the other. Quad-core Cortex-A35, 2GB RAM, 64GB storage, Wi-Fi, USB-C, speaker, dual mics, Android-based, supports TXT/PDF/EPUB/MOBI/HTML.

For: productivity workers, students, professionals, researchers, writers, readers who want a portable digital notebook with the feel of paper.
