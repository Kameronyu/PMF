| T-01-04 | Elevation (page JS exec) | fetch.js Playwright render | accept | Playwright renders page JS in a sandboxed headless Chromium (`--no-sandbox` is the container flag, not page-script trust); we read `page.content()` as a string. Local research tooling, proportionate risk. Inherits the existing crowdfund-fetch.js posture. |
