# COLD ADVERSARIAL AUDIT — Reviewer B (Prompt-Blind Grounding Audit, Arduview pipeline)

Findings the as-ran report already absorbs (asran §3/§9) and PART3 §1.1 are NOT re-flagged as new.

## SECTION 3 — VALIDATED: KEEP THESE (what Reviewer B found sound; the redesign must not regress)

Reviewer B's praise is a first-class deliverable. It validated four things as **sound discipline the old run actually exhibited**. Each carries a concrete regression risk created by the redesign.

1. **VALIDATED: No laundered demand upgrade — the integrity threads (cell, differentiator, demand-validation) survived all four handoffs.** Reviewer B: *"None that are laundered upgrades. The chain's core integrity threads (cell, differentiator, demand-validation) survive without silent hardening."* And Handoff 2: *"The deep-pass does NOT silently upgrade the chosen cell's demand… **This is the single most important non-drift in the whole chain and it holds.**"*
   - **REGRESSION RISK (highest-value watch).** R1 introduces a *pre-selection currency model* (§1.5 ripple #3: *"ad-count/spend/longevity per transformation/angle are currencies produced before selection"*) and a VOC demand currency (§5.2) wired into Gate 1. The old run resisted laundering partly *because it was dumb* — it had no demand signal in-cell (`validation_lane: unknown`, `qualifying_creatives: 0`) and honestly carried the nulls. The new design **manufactures demand currencies upstream of selection**. The `gap_candidates[]` mechanism (§5.1) explicitly surfaces *"themes with high frequency/intensity in VOC but no validated competitor spend"* — i.e. demand with **no supply-side validation**. If the whitespace-vs-scary policy (○, reserved) ever lets a VOC-only demand signal *count as demand currency in a Gate*, that is exactly the laundered-upgrade path the old run could not take. The design's stated guard is strong — §5.1: *"the pipeline must surface gaps with evidence on both sides, and must not auto-treat either side as a verdict"* (○) — but it is a **reserved policy, not yet a mechanism**. WATCH: Job 3/Job 4 must not let `voc_strength` alone clear a demand gate; the "evidence row" requirement (§6.5: *"every verdict carries its evidence row"*) must force BOTH the supply-side and demand-side cells to be populated, or a verdict can be emitted on the demand cell alone.
   - Second regression vector: §6.5 refuse condition is *"`voc-market-signal` absent when the run declares VOC active"* with *"soft-gate mode… as an explicit operator override."* The as-ran D-08 soft-gate is exactly how the old run carried Gate-1 kills as flags. If soft-gate silently becomes the default again, the demand thread can be upgraded by override. The design says override must be *"never a silent default"* — keep it that way.

2. **VALIDATED: The exclusion discipline — gameshell carried as cross-cell STRUCTURE reference, never as in-cell evidence.** Reviewer B: *"gameshell is from a DIFFERENT cell (maker-identity), so its belief records are valid as structure-reference only, NOT as in-cell evidence — and the deep-pass / FUNNEL-DESIGN correctly label it Currency-B structure, not in-cell fill. **Thread handled honestly.**"* And: the comparable-bet-seed exclusion (nothing-phone) was *"GROUNDED. Space-map combo… naming nothing-phone among seeds."*
   - **REGRESSION RISK.** R1 dissolves the light pass and makes the Space Map a synthesizer over *the full verified roster* (§1.5 STEP 2-3). The cross-cell/Currency-B labeling that kept gameshell honest depended on the as-ran two-currency `funnel-score.js` + `validation_lane` discipline (asran §2c). The redesign keeps this ("Structure references (Currency B)"), but the **never-merge rule that enforced it is now declared *configurable, not hard-coded*** (§6.7: *"The three-layer authority model / never-merge rule becomes a declared, configurable policy input… the D5 session decides its fate"*; §2 found it *"has no KB basis"*). WATCH: if the D5 session weakens never-merge to permit "awareness-matched messaging on crowdfunding structure," the clean separation that let gameshell be structure-only could collapse into treating a cross-cell crowdfunding raise as in-cell demand. P16 (operator: *"is the goal to NEVER merge, what does that even mean… i want to challenge this a bit"*) is the operator opening exactly this door — the audit's job is to flag that the door, once opened, is the seam where B's praised exclusion discipline can regress.

3. **VALIDATED: The differentiator never silently hardened — "ownable-but-unproven, cosmetic-only" survived intact.** Reviewer B, Thread 4 (its **"Strongest-surviving thread"**): *"the differentiator never silently hardens from 'ownable-but-unproven-and-cosmetic' into 'proven functional advantage.' The COPY's 'no over-claim check passed: transparency framed as aesthetic only' closes the loop."* And: *"Transparency-as-a-verbal-hook: unproven in paid media by anyone… Licensed and faithfully repeated, including the uncertainty."*
   - **REGRESSION RISK.** The uncertainty flag rode through the old chain as free-text discipline ("the single biggest live-test unknown"). The redesign converts judgments to structured `basis` fields and currency rows. Structured fields are good, but a `bet_type`/`mechanism` basis field plus the new functional-mechanism-equivalence test in the Bet Compiler (§6.1: *"does the physically-different feature do the same believable job for the same transformation"*) creates a path to **promote transparency from "ownable-but-unproven verbal hook" to "validated mechanism-type"** via the P19 bet-transfer logic (operator: *"if theres another rare piece that achieves this same exact thing believably… this mechanism type DOES work and we have a unique one… that's actually one of the best signals"*). That logic is sound, but it is precisely a mechanism for *upgrading* an unproven differentiator on the strength of a *different* product's validation. WATCH: the mechanism-vs-feature believability gate (§7.3 slot, A16/A18 — *"must pass a believability gate"*) must keep "transparency as verbal hook" labeled unproven-in-paid even after a functional-equivalence match licenses the *bet*; the bet being transferable must not silently re-label the *verbal hook* as proven. The §6.4 "aesthetic-only, no functional over-claim" guardrail and the Copy Chief believability test (§6.9 #2) are the enforcement points to keep.

4. **VALIDATED: The funnel reads competitor trust moves and refuses the ones a debut can't claim; ports stances not functional claims.** Reviewer B: *"the funnel correctly identifies these as competitor assets a debut brand CANNOT claim and blocks them. **Exemplary**"*; and on Pocket Operator: *"the funnel correctly identifies it and explicitly refuses to port the functional claim because Arduview's transparency is cosmetic. The one watch: blocked-ports are passed *in the brief* from the architect; if the architect's input set (§6.7, closed list) plus GAP-4's adjacent-market funnels feed it competitor trust assets without the "debut-cannot-claim" filter that the as-ran run applied by hand, an adjacent-market funnel's scale-stat/press-logo move could leak into the brief as portable. Keep the "permanently blocked: Available at Amazon, press logos, backer count, scale stats" discipline as an explicit auditor check, not an architect courtesy.

### Auditor's bottom line
Reviewer B's central finding — **no laundered upgrades, integrity threads intact** — means the redesign inherits a *clean* chain, so the prosecutable question flips from "what did they fix" to "what can they break." The two genuinely LIVE structural gaps are (1) the out-of-chain offer/deposit/asset dependency (needs a scope ruling) and (2) the above-band `$299` anchor with no forcing test (needs a registry slot). The naked-assertion CLASS that Reviewer B's whole method centers on is **not converted, only relocated**: the redesign's promise of "per-property `basis` fields universally" (§6.4) and "every verdict carries its evidence row" (§6.5) moves "asserted-it-will-be-grounded" from stripped fields to the synthesizer, the currency model, and the offer step — and a `basis`/evidence-row filled with a non-licensing datum is still a naked assertion. Whether that promise actually grounds anything depends entirely on the ○ Job 2 test content writing **refuse-conditions**, not just slots. The most important thing to protect is the demand-validation thread Reviewer B singled out as "the single most important non-drift": the new pre-selection demand currencies and VOC `gap_candidates[]` are the first real path this pipeline has ever had to launder a demand upgrade, and only a reserved-not-yet-built policy stands in the way.
