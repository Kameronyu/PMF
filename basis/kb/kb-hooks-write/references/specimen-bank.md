# Specimen bank — verbatim winning hooks, with the angle each executes

Preserved verbatim from `hooks--spencer-origins.md` / `hooks--mark-builds-brands.md`.
These are the highest-value asset in the skill — study and adapt, do not abstract into
templates. Each specimen shows the *form* of a good answer; that is what steers a model.

## Push-Pull (alarming negative → immediate positive resolution)
- "I think I just got scammed by every supplement brand for 10 years — until I found this."
  — angle: betrayal/skepticism of the category → this product as the exception.
- "The weird side effect nobody talks about from [product category] is actually [desirable outcome]."
  — angle: unexpected-benefit reframe (template-shaped in source; fill with a real category + outcome).

## New-Mechanism + Authority Stack
- "The hollow fiber revolution that's making cardiologists trash their own compression socks happened completely by accident."
  — angle: unfamiliar mechanism + authority abandoning their own tool + accidental-discovery loop.

## Direct angle→hook (direct beats indirect, especially at scale)
- "14 hours, no swelling"            — angle: reduces swelling.
- "This is a meal, not a protein shake" — angle: fills like a meal.
- "5 minutes to install, no more snapping" — angle: stops snapping.

## Ease-Based (minimal effort + absence of friction; needs a believable mechanism)
- "No toothbrush needed... try this for 30 seconds daily."

## Behavior-Specific headlines (what the avatar is visibly DOING; visceral verbs)
- "Constantly chugging water instead of eating"
- "Eating toddler-sized portions but still gaining weight"
- "Constantly chewing gum to avoid eating"
  — these are the highest-performing type for sub-avatars; generate multiple variants per behavior.

## Negative-curiosity (information gap, not a direct threat — for solution/product-aware)
- "The reason your collagen supplements aren't working (and what actually does)"
  — contrast with the fear-hook version for problem-aware: "Your skin is under attack."

> Note: AI can generate sub-avatar hooks at scale but needs the **avatar profile** as
> input — a product brief alone yields generic output. Feed real VOC, not adjectives.
