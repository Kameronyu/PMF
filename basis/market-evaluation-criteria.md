# Optimal Market Characteristics — Evaluation Framework

## Criteria by Category

### Desire
- Permanent human desire? Close to core: health, status, sex, comfort, belonging
- Social listening signal?
- Severity? (intensity × emotional charge × frequency of experiencing the pain)
- Purchase signal? Are people spending money on this transformation anywhere?
- Scope? How many people feel it
- Staying power / LTV? Will they keep coming back, can you sell a consumable?
- Attainable? Can a mechanism plausibly deliver it? (Scite AI, Web of Science)
- Can you catch an early revolutionary transformation and commercialize in D2C?

### Avatar
- High purchasing power?
- Do they identify with the problem?
- Have they bought for this problem before? (Awareness stage indicator)
- Unsatisfied with current solutions?
- No brand loyalty?
- VOC accessible? (Active Reddit/Facebook groups, Amazon reviews)
- Giftable? (Care signaling market available)

### Economics
- AOV ceiling: max this avatar pays for this transformation?
- COGS floor: can a plausible D2C mechanism be formulated under that ceiling?
- High margins? (80%+ / 5x COGS)
- Easy to ship? (Shoebox rule)
- Shippable from China with acceptable delivery time?

### Access Channel
- Are they reachable on Meta?
- What other access channels?

### Competitive State
- Low sophistication / no clear category dominator? (Not many competing offers, nobody owns it)
- Proven purchase signal on paid? (Competitor doing $500K+ — Mark's criterion)
- Competitors NOT scaling on Meta? (Opportunity to be first on that channel)

### Mechanism State
- Commercializable? Already a product? Can it be made into a consumer product?
- Feasible to invent?
- Patented?
- Can multiple mechanisms stack into one protocol?

### Awareness / Funnel Complexity
- Self-evident UM or needs education? (Single scale: image ad → PDP at one end, VSL → advertorial → PDP at the other)

### Temporal
- Desire adoption velocity? (Is the transformation becoming more wanted?)
- Mechanism adoption velocity? (Are early adopters starting to use it? Citation spikes? Twitter influencers?)
- Evergreen or seasonal? (Entering during peak?)
- Regulatory issues?

---

## Solving Order (rough draft)

1. **What market?** Start with a desire.
   - Is the desire strong?
   - Is the market for this desire growing?
   - Plausibly solvable with commercial products? (don't pick curing cancer)

2. **Competitive landscape.** Who does what?
   - Who's playing
   - How much money do they make
   - How sophisticated are they

3. **Mechanism.** What could we sell to this market?

4. **Economic viability.**

5. **VOC research.**
